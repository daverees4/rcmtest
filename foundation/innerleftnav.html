Apply to the RCM

This section takes you through everything you need to think about when you consider applying to the RCM, including:

  • Coming along to an open day
  • The minimum entry requirements for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes
  • How to apply for undergraduate and postgraduate programmes via UCAS Conservatoires
  • Information about auditions and audition venues
  • Details of prescribed audition pieces
  • Information about fees and funding, including scholarships and awards

You can also view the information according to the course you wish to study. Click on the revelant course in the boxes on the left or the right of this page.

You should find answers to most of your questions here or on our FAQ page, but if you need to know anything else please contact us on 020 7591 4362 or admissions@rcm.ac.uk and we'll be very pleased to hear from you.
